How Much Would You Spend?

The 12 Year Transportation Program covers projects for all transportation scenarios that can be funded within budget. Pennsylvania’s current 12 Year Program budget is around $62 billion. The budget below is set to 2017 program funding. For this exercise you have a budget of $100. Balance your budget to be successful. Each scenario has a minimum funding level that's necessary to keep the system performing (indicated by a darker shade).

  1. Increase or decrease each gauge to show how much you would spend for each scenario.
  2. You cannot spend less than the minimum.
  3. The budget amount will adjust based on your selections.
  4. Once you have a balanced budget, select the "Continue Survey" button.
